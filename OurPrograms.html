The Cardio

Tabata Sessions

Tabata is a form of high-intensity interval training (HIIT) that consists of 20 seconds of all-out effort followed by 10 seconds of rest, repeated for 8 rounds. This efficient workout maximizes calorie burn and cardiovascular benefits in a short period. Perfect for those looking to challenge themselves and achieve quick results
